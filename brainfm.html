Patented Technology

Brain.fm holds patients on key proccesses for creating functional music, including technology to elicit strong neutral phase locking- allowing populations of neurons to engage in varous kinds of coordinated activity- and technology to remove distraction in sound.

This makes our music unique, purpose-built to steer you into a desired mental state. In other words, we'vefound new ways to create music that helps you do what you need to do.

Tested on Brain and Behaviour

Scientists at Brain.fm work with collaborators at academic insitutions to run experiements.

We look at the effects of our technology on the bain, using fMRI and EEG, and also run large-scale behavioral tests, measuring performance on simple games.

Our experiements always include a control condition ('placebo') of the same underlying music without Brain.fm technology applied, so we can be sure our tech is what makes the difference.
